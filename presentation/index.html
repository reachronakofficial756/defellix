🧾 Section 65B Certificate

Under the Indian Evidence Act, electronic records are admissible in court only with a Section 65B certificate — certifying the record's authenticity and integrity.

Defellix automatically generates this certificate using the blockchain hash as proof that the contract was never altered. What used to require a lawyer and days of work — is now instant, free, and legally binding.
